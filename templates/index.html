    Method

    Built for transparent pricing exploration.

    The app trains a product-wise regression model, validates it on a holdout split, and scans candidate price points to estimate demand and revenue tradeoffs.

    Input design

    Price is modeled directly, while products receive their own offset and price interaction terms.

    Optimization logic

    Each product is evaluated over a realistic price band to identify the highest modeled revenue point.

    Use case

    Best suited for exploratory analysis, academic demos, and early-stage pricing recommendation systems.
